About

Buy up a portfolio of shares, click on "New Season", and see how your portfolio has grown (or shrunk) as dividends and share values fluctuate.

Dividends will be between 1% and 10% of a shares current value; no dividend is given if a share falls in value.

There is no admin fee for buying shares, but a 1.5% fee for selling (or cashing in your shares), and a 2% annual management fee on your entire portfolio (excluding your cash balance.
